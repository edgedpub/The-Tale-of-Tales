Third Entertainment of the Third Day

Renza is enclosed in a tower by her father, since it was predicted that she would die from a big bone. She falls in love with a prince and, with a bone brought to her by a dog, makes a hole in the wall and flees. But when she later sees her beloved kiss the bride he has since married, she dies of a broken heart, and out of sorrow he pince kills himself.
