IXThe three citrons

Ninth Entertainment of the Fifth Day

Ciommettiello does not want a wife, but when hu cuts his finger over some ricotta he gets the desire for one with a white and red complexion, like that of the mixture of ricotta and blood. And thus he wanders through the world, and at the Island of Three Fairies receives three citrons. When he cuts one of these he obtains a beautiful fairy who conforms to is heart’s desire; after she is killed by a slave he takes the black girl in place of the white one. This betrayal is discovered and the slave is made to die, and the fairy, who returns to life, becomes queen.
