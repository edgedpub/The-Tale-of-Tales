VISapia

Sixth Entertainment of the Fifth Day

Sapia, the daughter of a great baroness, makes a wise man out of the king’s son Carluccio, who hadn’t have been able to learn the alphabet. When he receives a slap on the cheek from Sapia, Carluccio decides to take revenge by marrying her, and after a thousand torments he has three babies with her and, without realizing it, they are reconciled.
