Sixth Entertainment of the Third Day

Because she obeys her father by doing his pleasure and conducts herself shrewdly in what she has been ordered to do, Belluccia, daughter of Ambruoso of Barra, is most richly married to Narduccio, the firstborn of Basilio Guallecchia, which results in her poor sisters being supplied with dowries and given as wives to his other sons.
