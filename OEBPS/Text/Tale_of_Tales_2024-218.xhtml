VIThe Three Crowns

Sixth Entertainment of the Fourth Day

Marchetta is abducted by the wind and carried off to the house of an ogress, from whom, after various events, she receives a slap. She departs, dressed as a man, and ends up in the house of a king, where the queen falls in love with her and, resentful that her love is not returned, accuses her in front of her husband of attempting to disgrace her. Marchetta is condemned to hang, but due to the power of a ring given to ther by the ogress she is freed, and when her accuser is killed she becomes queen.
