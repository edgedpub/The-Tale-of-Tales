IXRosella

Eighth Entertainment of the Third Day

The Grand Turk has a prince captured so that he can bathe in a lord’s blood. His daughter falls in love with the prince and they run away, but the girl’s mother comes after them and and her hands are cut off by the prince. The Grand Turk dies of a broken heart; when his daughter is put under a curse by her mother the prince forgets about her. But after the girl performs some trickery, she returns to her husband’s memory and they live happily ever after.
